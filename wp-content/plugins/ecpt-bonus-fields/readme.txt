================ INSTALLATION ================

To install Easy Content Types: Bonus Meta Field Types, simply upload the unzipped folder called "ecpt-bonus-fields" to your wp-content/plugins directory.

Next, navigate to your WordPress plugins menu and click "Activate" on "Easy Content Types: Bonus Meta Field Types".

All of the additional field types (and their respective short codes) will now be available.

================ USAGE =======================

For your convenience, all usage documentation has been included inside WordPress.

Click on the "Bonus Fields Help" link inside the "Content Types" menu.
