================ INSTALLATION ================

To install Restrict Content Pro, simply upload the unzipped folder called "restrict-content-pro" to your wp-content/plugins directory.

Next, navigate to your WordPress plugins menu and click "Activate" on "Restrict Content Pro".

You will now have a new menu item called "Restrict". This is where you will setup your subscription levels and manage memberships.

================ USAGE =======================

For your convenience, all usage documentation has been included inside WordPress.

Click on the "Help" link inside the "Restrict" menu.
